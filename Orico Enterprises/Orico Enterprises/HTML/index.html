About Us

Orico Enterprises is the sister concern of Engineering Air Products was founded by a group of engineers in 2005. Our company aims at providing the luxury of comfort with our high-end and technologically advanced products. We always adapt to the most recent technology and always surprise our customers with top-notch products.
